Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2.

Most people infected with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older adults and people who have severe underlying medical conditions like heart or lung disease or diabetes seem to be at higher risk for developing more serious complications from COVID-19 illness.

Please refer to the World Health Organization (WHO) or the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) for more information pertaining to COVID-19.
